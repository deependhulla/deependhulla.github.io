Promotional Email Tagging plugin for SpamAssassin [July 2017]

Tired of being bombarded with promotional emails in your work mailbox? This plugin is here to help. It tags those pesky ads, newsletters, and bulk marketing messages that often cause irritation. Once tagged, you can easily filter them using Dovecot Sieve or MS-Outlook client mail-filter, effortlessly moving them to a separate folder.
